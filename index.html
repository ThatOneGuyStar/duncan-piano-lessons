About Me

Hello! I'm Elijah Duncan, a passionate pianist and dedicated educator. I am currently a high school student at Walter Johnson High School, where I am deeply immersed in music education. I am taking AP Music Theory, a college-level music theory class, and the highest level piano class offered by my school. I am also an active member of my symphonic band.

My teaching philosophy focuses on building a strong foundation in technique, theory, and musicality, while also tailoring lessons to each student's individual goals and interests. Whether you dream of playing classical masterpieces, jazz improvisations, or contemporary pop songs, I'm here to guide you.

Lesson Offerings

Beginner Piano

Start your musical journey with fundamental concepts: note reading, basic chords, rhythm, and simple melodies. Perfect for absolute beginners with no prior experience.

Intermediate & Advanced

For students with some experience, we'll refine your technique, explore advanced repertoire, delve into music theory, and develop your unique musical voice.

Music Theory & Composition

Understand the language of music. Learn harmony, counterpoint, form, and even begin composing your own pieces to deepen your musical understanding and enhance improvisation.
